# Research Process

Standard process for research tasks on the team.

---

## Steps

1. Create a new page in the Research Hub (`research/`) with the topic name
2. Define the question you're trying to answer
3. Document your sources and findings
4. Write a clear conclusion and recommendation
5. Share findings with the team before building

---

## Research Page Template

```markdown
# [Topic Name]

**Question:** What are we trying to find out?

**Sources:** Links and references

**Findings:** What did you learn?

**Conclusion:** What do we do with this?
```
